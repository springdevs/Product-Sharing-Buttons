Share your zest with your friends and others.

 
## Features
 
1. Facebook Share
2. Twitter Share
3. Linkdin Share 
4. YouTube Share 
5. and Share with any Social network 
 
== Installation ==

    = Installation from within WordPress =

        1. Visit 'Plugins > 'Add New'.
        2. Search for 'WCMA Social Sharing Button'.
        3. Install and activate the 'WCMA Social Sharing Button' plugin.

    = Manual installation =

        1. Upload the entire `WCMA Social Sharing Button` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
        2. Visit 'Plugins'.
        3. Activate the `WCMA Social Sharing Button` plugin.
